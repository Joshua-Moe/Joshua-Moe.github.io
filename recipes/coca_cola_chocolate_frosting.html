This is a recipe for "Coca-Cola Chocolate Frosting"

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup (8 tbsp.) butter
  • 1/3 cup Coca-Cola
  • 2 tbsp. Hersheyâ€™s Cocoa Special Dark
  • One box (Net Wt 1 lb.) Domino Confectioners Sugar
  • 1 tsp. McCormick Premium Vanilla Flavor

Directions

  1. Melt butter in a large saucepan. Then add cocoa and Coca-Cola.
  2. Heat to boiling and immediately remove from the heat.
  3. Add confectioners sugar and vanilla flavor.
  4. Use electric mixer to blend well. The mixture will be fairly thin.
  5. Pour this mixture over the cake while the cake is hot.
  6. Let the cake and frosting cool. The frosting thickens as it cools.
  7. Enjoy.
