This is a recipe for "Spicy_Maple_Candied_Bacon"

Ingredients

  • 0.5 cup of pure maple syrup
  • 0.25 cup of light brown sugar
  • Optional - 0.5 tsp. of Cayenne Pepper
  • One pound of THICK CUT bacon

Directions

  1. Line a 4-sided baking sheet with Reynolds Wrap NONSTICK Aluminum Foil.
  2. Put the maple syrup, light brown sugar, and (optional) Cayenne Pepper into a bowl, and using a whisk, mix thoroughly.
  3. Dip each bacon strip in the above mixture and brush off any excess coating.
  4. Place the bacon strips onto the baking sheet so that they do not overlap.
  5. Place the baking sheet on the center rack of your oven and bake at 350° F. for about 30 minutes. (Your actual time will depend on the thickness of the bacon, and how crispy you want it done.)
  6. Place the bacon strips onto a cooling rack to cool.
  7. Enjoy.
