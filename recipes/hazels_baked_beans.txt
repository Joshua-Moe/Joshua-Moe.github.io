This is a recipe for "Hazel's Baked Beans"

Ingredients:

2 lb. of Food Lion Great Norhtern Beans.
2 cups of Food Lion Dark Brown Sugar.
1/4 cup + a little of Brer Rabbit Full Flavor Molasses unsulphured.
2 tsp. of salt.
1/4 tsp. of pepper.
1/2 lb. of Oscar Mayer Naturally Hardwood Smoked Bacon.

Directions:

1. Put the beans into a large Dutch oven, and cover them with about 2 inches of water.
	Soak the beans 4-5 hours or overnight.
2. Bring to a boil, and boil for 5 minutes.
3. Drain, and rinse the beans.
4. Put all of the ingredients into a large Dutch oven.
5. Add enough water so it is level with the top of the beans, and cover with a lid.
6. Preheat oven to 300Â°.
7. Bake for 5 hours.
8. Remove the lid from the Dutch oven, and bake for 1 more hour.
9. Enjoy.
