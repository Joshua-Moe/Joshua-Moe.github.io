This is a recipe for "Oven Fried Pickles"

Ingredients

  • Baking sheet
  • Reynolds Wrap heavy duty non-stick aluminum foil
  • 3 bowls
  • One 16 oz. jar of Mt. Olive Sandwich Stuffers Bread & Butter
  • ½ cup of Martha White All-Purpose Flour
  • ½ tsp. of salt
  • ¼ tsp. of pepper
  • 1 tsp. of garlic powder
  • 2 room temperature eggs beaten
  • 1 cup of 4C Bread Crumbs, Panko Plain
  • ½ tsp. of Cayenne Pepper

Directions

  1. Drain the pickles for a long time, and spread them out on papers towels. Pat them dry with more paper towels to remove as much moisture as possible.
  2. Put the all-purpose flour, salt, pepper, and garlic powder into bowl #1, and mix well.
  3. Beat the 2 eggs in bowl #2
  4. Put the bread crumbs and cayenne pepper into bowl #3.
  5. Pre heat the oven to 450° F.
  6. Cover baking sheet with aluminum foil.
  7. Using your clean fingers, coat each pickle thoroughly in bowl #1, bowl #2, bowl #3, and place it on the baking sheet.
  8. Bake for 20 to 25 minutes.
