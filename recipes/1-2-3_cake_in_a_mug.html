This is a recipe for "1-2-3 Cake in a Mug"

Ingredients

  • One 15.25 oz. package of Dunkin Hines Perfectly Moist Devil’s Food Cake Mix
  • One 16 oz. package of Dunkin Hines Perfectly Moist Angel Food Cake Mix

Directions

  1. Blend the 2 ingredients in an airtight container like a 6.2 cup Rubbermaid Take along, and store away until ready for dessert.
  2. Put 2 tbsp. of water and 3 tbsp. of the dry mixture into a large coffee mug. Stir well.
  3. Microwave on high for 1 minute.
  4. Top with fruit, ice cream, whipped cream, or my favorite, Hershey’s Genuine Chocolate Flavored Syrup.
  5. Enjoy
