This is a recipe for "Gary's Egg Salad Filling for Sandwiches"

Ingredients (for 2 sandwiches):

One hard-boiled egg.
Two tbsp. of Kraft Light Mayo.
Two tbsp. of Mt. Olive Hot Dog Relish.
Two to four slices of bread or toast.

Directions:

1. Mash the hard-boiled egg.
2. Mix in the Mayo and Hot Dog Relish.
3. Spread on 2 slices of bread or toast.
4. Enjoy.
