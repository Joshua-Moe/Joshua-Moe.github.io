This is a recipe for "Gary's Egg Salad"

Ingredients (for 2 sandwiches)

  • One hard-boiled egg.
  • Two tbsp. of Kraft Light Mayo.
  • Two tbsp. of Mt. Olive Hot Dog Relish.
  • Two to four slices of bread or toast.

Directions

  1. Put an egg into boiling water, and boil for 12 minutes.
  2. Put the hard-boiled egg into a bowl of ice water, and chill for 5 minutes.
  3. Shell and mash the hard-boiled egg.
  4. Mix in the Mayo and Hot Dog Relish.
  5. Spread on 2 slices of bread or toast.
  6. Enjoy.
