This is a recipe for "Hazel’s Søt Suppe (Norwegian for Sweet Soup)"

Ingredients

  • ½ lbs. of Sunsweet Amazin Pitted Prunes
  • 1 cup or more of Sun Maid Raisins
  • 1 lemon sliced (Wash and remove price sticker before slicing. Remove seeds after slicing.)
  • ¾ to 1 cup of sugar
  • ½ cup of Kraft Minute Tapioca (See Direction #4)

Directions

  1. Rinse the prunes.
  2. Put the prunes, raisins, lemon slices, and sugar into a Dutch oven. Cover these ingredients with about 2 inches of water.
  3. Bring to a boil, then turn down the heat, cover, and simmer for 45 minutes stirring occasionally
  4. Shake the box of tapioca, measure, and add slowly while stirring so it doesn’t clump.
  5. Simmer for another 15 minutes stirring occasionally.
  6. Let cool.
  7. Enjoy.
