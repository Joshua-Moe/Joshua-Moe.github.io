This is a recipe for "Hazel's Taco Seasoning"

Ingredients

  • 6 tsp. of chili powder.
  • 4 1/2 tsp. of ground cumin.
  • 5 tsp. of paprika.
  • 3 tsp. of onion powder.
  • 2 1/2 tsp. of garlic powder.
  • 1/4 to 1/2 tsp. of ground cayenne red pepper.

Directions

  1. Mix and store in air tight container.
  2. Enjoy. This is twice as strong as the store bought.
