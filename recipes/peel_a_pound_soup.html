This is a recipe for "Peel-A-Pound Soup"

(Ref. YouTube video "How to lose 15 pounds in 7 days with Peel-a-Pound Soup")

Ingredients

  • One 28 oz. can of stewed tomatoes OR whole peeled tomatoes.
  • 1 packet of Lipton Recipe Secret Onion Soup Mix.
  • 6-7 beef bouillon cubes in 3 cups of water.
  • 1 red onion coarse chopped.
  • 1 white onion coarse chopped.
  • 1 green bell pepper chopped.
  • 1 red bell pepper chopped.
  • 2 stalks of celery chopped.
  • 1 garlic chopped.
  • 1 head of green cabbage coarse chopped.

Directions

  1. Put the ingredients into a Dutch oven in the order that they are listed above, stirring as you go.
  2. Bring this to a boil, and add in any remaining ingredients as it cooks down. stir occasionally.
  3. Turn the heat down, put a lid on it, and let it simmer until the veggies are as tender as you like. Stir occasionally. This step may take 1-2 hours.
